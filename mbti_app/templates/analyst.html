Recommendation

Prepare an industry analysis and focus on selling the concept of your product, rather than going into details about your product. Wizards tend to like discussing concepts rather than be bogged down by details. Occasionally, they do ask for details, but it is usually so that they can do their own analysis. Be prepared to be challenged, and explain with logical reason why your product is preferred.
