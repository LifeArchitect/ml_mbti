Approach

With the Explorers, you don't have to be too serious. Be friendly, easy-going and casual and make some small talk with them. They're most open to you when things are light and easy.

Recommendation

During the pitch, keep it short and keep them engaged by having a dialogue. Explorers tend to disengage quickly if they are asked to just sit down and listen to a presentation. If possible, make the presentation appealing to all the five senses - when they experience a product or service and they like it, nothing much else has to be said. They are already likely to buy.
