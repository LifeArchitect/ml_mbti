Approach

The first step to take in connecting with the Healer is to build a personal relationship. Don't focus on trying to sell from the start - rather make effort to connect by being genuinely interested in them. Show sincerity in your interest.

Recommendation

Share your personal belief in the product you are selling and communicate the passion you have for the product. Even though your passion may be different from theirs, they enjoy seeing someone who is deeply certain about what they stand for. If it is relevant, talk about your product elevants the mental, emotional and spiritual well-being of others - Healers, as their name suggests, want to see the world become better and humanity lifted up.

Sales Script

The People Mastery system promotes greater self-understanding and greater understanding between people. I’m going to help each individual recognize their unique value as a salesperson and increase their self-esteem. After all, being confident in who you are is an important first step to succeeding in life. It’s a great tool for self-discovery as people begin to learn more about themselves. I believe that as there is greater self-awareness, there will be better communication and we can have more harmony in the workplace, community and the world. I hope to bring this to every household and office, so that there is less conflict, and more love to go around.

Actionable steps

  • Making the world a better place via greater understanding
  • Help each individual recognize their unique value and increase their self-esteem
  • A tool for self-discovery
  • Greater acceptance between people of the world
  • Improve workplace communication and harmony
